About this site

This site is a hub for useful advice and references to sources that will help you be a good academic writer. Here, you will also find information on the publication process and what you might expect, in addition to useful hints and links to resources. We will help you 'debunk' the mysteries of academic publishing and, finally, you will also find tips for how to identify and avoid the 'pirates' out there, the predatory publishers and conference organisers. It is a bumby, long and windy road to publish academic work, but starting here, you have already improved your chances for(hopefully) getting published.

50 years of experience

So, what do we know? Why should you listen to us? Between us we have more than 50 years of experience from academic publishing in various top international journals and books. We have adviced young as well as senior scholars on all aspects of publication, from good writing to how to promote your published article in social media. We have experience from working closely with different publishers and been engaged in every step of the publication process and production aspects. In addition, most of us have published ourselves and some of us extensively.

We know what it takes!
